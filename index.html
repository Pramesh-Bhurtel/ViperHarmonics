MIDI Integration

Plug-and-play support for any MIDI controller. Maps 1:1 with standard key frequencies. Enjoy authentic harmonium response with full MIDI velocity support.

Performance Sharing

Every performance is serialized into a compact URL. Share your favorite rags or melodies instantly via the built-in QR generator or direct social links. Recipients can play back your jam instantly with zero setup.

ViperHarmonics Engine

ViperHarmonics is a high-fidelity, pure software recreation of a classical Indian Harmonium. Leveraging the Web Audio API, it uses authentic reed samples coupled with real-time digital signal processing.

Audio Synthesis

Our engine combines high-resolution PCM sampling with subtractive synthesis to deliver an organic "living" sound. Includes mechanical key-click logic and velocity-mapped resonance.

Spatial Processing

The Ambience engine uses convolution reverb with captured impulse responses to place your performance in an authentic acoustic space, from dry studios to vast halls.
